Philippines is a fantastic place for anyone who loves different kinds of beautiful scenery. People enjoy this country's view because of its appealing places such as Boracay, Palawan, and many more. Here are 3 places you should definitely check out!

Boracay

  • A famous tourist attraction for both Filipinos and international visitors
  • has a 4 kilometers of clean white sand and turquoise sea
  • offers a limitless variety of aquatic sports

Banaue Rice Terraces, Ifugao

  • The Ifugao people carved it onto the rugged mountainside nearly 2,000 years ago.
  • Made with basic tools and hand labor
  • has a breathetaking view, especially during sunrises and sunsets

Mayon Volcano, Albay, Bicol

  • an active stratovolcano in the province of Albay in the Bicol Region of the Philippines
  • known as the "The World's Most Perfect Cone."
